Flapjax

Flapjax was the first JavaScript-based research project from the Brown PLT group. We were exploring ways to express the inherently event-driven nature of client-side web applications. We produced several running systems on top of Flapjax, and its ideas provided inspiration for several other frameworks (like Meteor.js).
